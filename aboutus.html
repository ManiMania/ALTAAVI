INTRODUCTION

Altaavi Electromechanical was founded in 2022 in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, with a vision to excel in MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) contracting. In a short span, we have established ourselves as one of the leading subcontracting companies in the U.A.E., recognized for our commitment to excellence and innovation. Our expertise extends beyond subcontracting to delivering exceptional recruitment solutions. We connect talented professionals with forward-thinking companies, ensuring a seamless match that benefits both the employee and the employer. By focusing on quality, privacy, and alignment with organizational culture, we empower businesses with skilled manpower that drives success. As an approved and authorized subcontracting company by the Ministry of Labor and licensed by the Department of Economic Development, Altaavi Electromechanical operates with the highest standards of compliance and professionalism. Our proven methodology and quick turnaround times make us the preferred choice for staffing and subcontracting needs across various industries. At Altaavi Electromechanical, we are dedicated to building strong partnerships, delivering outstanding value, and making a positive impact in every project we undertake.
