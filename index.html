Who We Are?

Altaavi Electromechanical was established in 2022 in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, and has quickly risen to prominence as one of the leading subcontracting companies in the U.A.E. Specializing in MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) contracting, we are recognized for our excellence and reputation in the region
As a recruitment specialist, Altaavi Electromechanical bridges the gap between talented candidates and forward-thinking companies. We pride ourselves on offering reliable and efficient recruitment solutions that empower businesses with skilled and motivated employees while aligning with the company's culture and goals.

Our Vision & Mission

To be the leading partner for businesses across industries by delivering unparalleled manpower solutions that drive growth and success.

To connect employers with top-tier talent through a focused and efficient recruitment process. To ensure candidates receive the right guidance to excel in their careers. To foster a culture of quality, privacy, and integrity in every recruitment initiative.

Our Motto

“BUILDING DREAMS” Our philosophy revolves around our vision of “Converting Dream Projects into Reality” and our aim is complete Client Satisfaction.
